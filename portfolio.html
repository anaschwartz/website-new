Remember

Steel Fabrication and Video Projection, 2 min 18 sec, 2023

In this video installation piece, I explored how the mind operates. Much like a playground--our minds deteriorate over time. Our memories become misconstrued or even fabricated. My video is a mashup of some of my childhood home videos. While I can recall a few of these clips, I only recognize some. Our minds pick and choose what to remember, sometimes in the form of bits and pieces, like only audio or visuals. Although bittersweet, it is inescapable.

I'm Here Now

Video, 6 min 4 sec, 2023

Short films have the difficult objective of having a lasting impression in a short amount of time. As a fan of horror, I wanted to take on this challenge myself. I also had the extra challenge of producing this on a $0 budget. All that's needed for a compelling film is passion and a vision. In this film, the protagonist has a consistent routine that deteriorates over time, as he feels something is not right. Someone or something is following him and it's getting closer.
